Chubb Rebrand

Trillions of dollars are expected to flow into Index Funds and ETFs in the coming years. However, Investors and Advisors are struggling to keep up with the flood of new products and, more critically, do not understand how to best utilize them in a portfolio.

I worked with FTSE Russell to help speed up this shift from active to passive investing by designing a platform that better contexualized these new products, bringing together real-time fund data, market commentary and news, and real-world ETF-based portfolios.

Working with the Global Head of Product, the CMO and teams across the company, I developed the product strategy, created a new brand and visual identity, defined and designed an MVP, engaged with launch partners and advertisers, and worked with product development teams to launch the new platform.

Client: FTSE Russell
Role: Product & Creative Lead
Output: Strategy Brief, Product Design Brief, Features & Functionality, Brand Identity, Visual Design, Prototypes, MVP
